Strictly Skateboarding
Designer & Developer

Overview

Strictly Skateboarding is an iOS application that connects Bournemouth’s skateboarding community through a social and location based platform.

View Website

Case Study

Strictly Skateboarding aims to provide the Bournemouth skateboarding community with a social and location-based platform. The application works to develop ties with the skateboarding community and promote the characteristics of the skateboarding culture.

Strictly Skateboarding offers new and fundamental features that are unique to skateboarding applications. Research showed that skateboarders were finding it increasingly difficult to discover new skateboarding locations and connect with fellow skateboarders. Strictly Skateboarding was built to solve this problem.

The main location feature within the application provides skateboarders with verified skateboarding locations or the option to create and edit their own. The social features within the application, such as posting images or sharing locations, build relationships and develop a community centred around the application.

User Interface

The user interface was carefully deliberated throughout the design process. The interface was designed to reflect the skateboarding brand image and carefully target the correct demographic and user. The simplistic black and red colour scheme highlighted by a bold typeface is identifiable with skateboarding. The design indicates the urban and gritty nature of the sport and translates a brand to product.

There is a consistent structure to the application that clearly communicates features through logical icons and visual hierarchy. The careful placement of items and specific use of colour draws and directs attention throughout and visually communicates a path for the user. The user interface has a sense of familiarity which puts the user at ease and allows them to comfortably explore the application. The application is kept simple whilst providing powerful and complex features.
