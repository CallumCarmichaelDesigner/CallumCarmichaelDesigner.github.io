About Me

I am a User Interface and Experience Designer who recently graduated from Bournemouth University with a 1st Class BA Honours degree in Digital Media Design. I have always been passionate and motivated by design, teaching myself visual effects at a young age and expanding my skillset into motion graphics until finally learning app development at University.

I strive to create beautiful yet functional products whilst expanding my knowledge of emerging technologies. My ambition is to make complex interfaces approachable, designing innovative and elegant experiences across responsive mobile and web devices. In developing and designing applications I have focussed on research, wireframing, prototyping and usability testing in order to optimise user experience and produce successful products. I am eager to use these skills in an energetic and motivated team, where I can produce original and exciting projects whilst growing my own technical abilities.
